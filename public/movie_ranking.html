The Best Movies from different genre in telugu film industry according to GEETHIKA

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bahubhali-2

This is my favourite Action Drama.Right from the word go, it has intense drama and action as every frame in the film is a visual wonder. Be it the heroic fights or the lavish war. A big apppreciation to the director Rajamouli for making this epic story whice gave good exposure for telugu film industry in the world.

Khushi

Its a feel good love Story. The story line and hero Pawan Kalyan's attitude in the film is irreplaceable.

Jersey

The bonding between father and son are the highlights of the film which makes audience to feel emotional and connected.

Rangasthalam

Its a periodic revenge drama which has good technical aspects that gives audience good experience to watch. The cast of the film gave thier best in the film.

AWE!

AWE is a scientific triller and a crafty anthology movie which relies too heavily on the strength of its climax. Too psychological, too poetic at times.
